Sneak Robber is a Student Capstone Unity3D game project designed and developed by Yubing Xie, Han Bu, Yuhan Liu, Change Ge.

Instructions to play Sneak Robber
Requirements: This is a three-person game. This game requires two screens and three controllers connected to one computer.
Instructions:	
1. Set up three controllers and two screens to PC or Mac.
2. Open the proper executable, PC or Mac version for your computer.
3. On main menu, move controller around to see which character each players are controlling.
4. If no "missing controller" message is displayed on screen, continue to play.
5. If hit "Play" on main menu, the ghost player should look at the screen for ghost tutorial and thief players should look at the screen for thief tutorial.
6. Follow instructions in game.
